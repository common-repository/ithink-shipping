<strong>How to Integrate WC Vendors  with iThink Logistics?</strong>
<p>WC vendor is a marketplace that allows vendors to sell products just like etsy, Envato, eBay, or Amazon. It is a great option to build a multi vendor website that expands a business in the best way possible.</p>

<p>If you are an online vendor, looking forward to shipping your products, this simple step by step guide will help you to integrate the iThink Logistics shipping plugin with your store.</p>

<p>Read on...</p>


<strong>Step A. Start by setting up your Woocommerce store</strong>

<p>1. Login to the WooCommerce admin panel by entering your registered username and password.<br>
2. From the left side menu bar, click on plugins<br>
3. From the dropdown, select the Add new plugins option.<br>
4. Search for iThink Logistics Multivendor.<br>
5. Click on install now<br>
6. Once it is installed, click on the downloaded file to activate the same<br>
7. Add the secret API keys and click on “submit”.<br></p>
<p>Your iThink logistics plugin is now activated and ready to use.</p>

<strong>Step B. Now it's time for setting up and fulfilling the order in Vendor panel</strong>
<p>1. Log in to the vendor panel using your registered username and password.<br>
2. Go to the orders section.<br>
3. Click on “add fulfillment” to fulfil the given order.<br>
4. The order is now. Fulfilled. Refer to the image below.<br>
5. To generate a Shipping label, click on “shipping label” options from the orders page.<br>
6. Your shipping label will be automatically generated.<br></p>

<strong>Step C. Now it's time for setting up and viewing in iThink Platform</strong>

<p>1. Login to your iThink logistics account <br>
2. Go to my store<br>
3. Select on WooCommerce to view all the orders from WooCommerce.<br></p>

<p>That’s it! Your Wcommerce is now integrated with iThink Logistics, and you can keep track of all your order shipments easily.</p>
